VeeChat is an AI-powered chat application that allows users to interact with YouTube videos through a conversational interface. By analyzing video transcripts and using advanced language models, the app enables users to ask questions, get summaries, and explore key insights from any video. Instead of watching long videos to find specific information, users can simply chat with the video and receive quick, accurate answers.
